§ 01 / Hardware

Signal flow, left to right.

Topology

Commands travel left to right — from the iPhone, over BLE, into the Raspberry Pi's GPIO, through the L293D H-bridge, and out to the DC motor with its propeller. The IR sensor on the propeller closes the feedback loop back to the Pi for closed-loop RPM control.

§ 04 / Concurrency

Three threads. One state struct.

Model

ble_server runs a single-process GLib main loop. Three independent sources push events into it: D-Bus dispatches for BLE commands, a GLib timer for telemetry, and pigpio alert callbacks for IR pulses. All three converge on one shared motor_state struct — contention is avoided by keeping mutations short and single-threaded inside the main loop.

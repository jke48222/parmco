§ 01 / Members

Builders of record.

§ iOS · BLE server

Jalen Edusei

linkedin.com/in/jalen-edusei-07b3a0322

Jalen led the mobile side — both the native SwiftUI app using CoreBluetooth and the React Native + Expo build. He designed the BLE central role: scan logic, service and characteristic discovery, command writes, and the telemetry notify subscription that drives the live RPM display.

He also wrote the BLE GATT server (ble_server.c) — the GLib/GDBus D-Bus layer that registers the custom service with BlueZ, the parse_command() dispatch loop, the telemetry timer, and the NoInputNoOutput pairing agent that enables autonomous iOS reconnect without console interaction.

SwiftUI CoreBluetooth React Native BlueZ · GDBus C / GLib
§ Hardware · Firmware asm

Justin Moreno

linkedin.com/in/justin-moreno-50b4082b7

Justin owned the hardware — wiring the L293D H-bridge to the Pi, mounting the IR sensor on the motor propeller, and building the 12 V power rail. He validated every GPIO connection against the BCM2711 datasheet before powering the board.

He wrote motor_control.c with its three closed-loop modes, the proportional RPM controller, and the pigpio DMA PWM integration. He also authored gpio_asm.s — ARMv7 assembly performing direct BCM2711 GPIO register writes through memory-mapped /dev/gpiomem, following AAPCS32 conventions throughout.

L293D pigpio · DMA PWM ARMv7 asm BCM2711 Closed-loop
